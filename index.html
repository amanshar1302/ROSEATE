The Eras Tour

A Journey Through All The Eras

The Eras Tour is the most ambitious and celebrated concert tour in modern history, taking fans on an unforgettable journey through Taylor Swift's entire musical career. From country beginnings to pop anthems, from folklore's indie soundscapes to Midnights' electric energy, each era tells a story.

Experience over three hours of music, spanning 17 years of chart-topping hits, deep cuts, and surprise songs that have defined a generation. The tour has broken multiple records and become a cultural phenomenon.
